Persian Walnut Cookies

Prep Time:15 mins Cook Time:20 mins Additional Time:10 mins Total Time:45 mins

Servings:36 Yield:36 cookies

Persian Walnut Cookies

These rose-scented Persian cookies are delicately spiced. They're the perfect treat for Passover since they contain no flour. They are nutty, rich, and slightly chewy with a crunchy exterior.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups finely ground walnuts
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks, divided
  • 1 tablespoon ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon rose water
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 1/2 cup walnut pieces for decoration
