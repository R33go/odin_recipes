Banana Bread

Prep Time:10 mins Cook Time:1 hrs Total Time:1 hr 10 mins

Servings:15 Yield:1 9x5-inch loaf

Banana Bread

This is the moistest banana that I have ever tasted. Very delicous perfect to have with some coffee also very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped sour cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 medium bananas, sliced
